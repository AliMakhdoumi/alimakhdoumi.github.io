Ali Makhdoumi

Associate Professor
Duke University
Fuqua School of Business · Decision Sciences
Computer Science (secondary)

I received a BSc in Electrical Engineering and a BSc in Mathematics from Sharif University of Technology. Before joining Fuqua, I received my PhD in EECS from LIDS at MIT. I was lucky to be advised by Asuman Ozdaglar and Daron Acemoglu.

My research interests include optimization, game theory, networks, and learning with applications to market design, data markets, and privacy.
